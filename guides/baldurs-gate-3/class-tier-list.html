Pro Tip: The single most important piece of gear in the current meta is the Band of the Mystic Scoundrel (Act 3, Jungle area). This ring lets you cast Illusion/Enchantment spells as a bonus action after making a weapon attack. On a Swords Bard, this means: Attack 4 times → bonus action Hold Person (guaranteed crits on next round). On a Sorlock: Eldritch Blast 3 beams → bonus action Hold Monster → auto-crit beams next turn. This one item raises every hybrid build by a full tier.

Pro Tip: Don't sleep on initiative. In BG3, initiative is a d4 roll (not d20 like tabletop). This means dexterity bonuses are FOUR TIMES more impactful. A character with 16 Dexterity goes before one with 10 Dexterity on 85% of rolls. The Alert feat (+5 initiative, immune to surprise) is the single best feat in the game for any build on Honor Mode. Going first means one extra turn — in a game where most fights end in 2-3 rounds, that's 33-50% more damage.
