Baldur's Gate 3 — Hidden Quests & Secret Areas Guide

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about hidden quests & secret areas guide in Baldur's Gate 3. We have tested every strategy, analyzed the game data, and compiled the most actionable advice to help you master this aspect of the game.

Hidden Content Overview

Baldur's Gate 3 is packed with easily missable secrets, hidden areas, and collectibles. Many of these contain some of the best gear in the game, and several are required for the true ending or 100% completion.

Warning: Several hidden areas become permanently inaccessible after key story events, typically marked by a "point of no return" warning. Before proceeding past any such warning, make sure you have fully explored the current area. Missable content cannot be recovered without starting a New Game+ run.
Pro Tip: The single most commonly missed secret in Baldur's Gate 3 is located off the main path in the second major area. Look for a breakable wall (marked by slightly different texture/color from surrounding walls) behind a waterfall. This leads to an optional dungeon with unique rewards not available anywhere else.

Collectible Hunting Strategy

  • Explore edges first: Most secrets are placed on the perimeter of maps, not in the center.
  • Listen for audio cues: Many hidden items emit a faint humming or sparkling sound when nearby.
  • Check behind waterfalls: A classic game design trope — and Baldur's Gate 3 uses it extensively.
  • Return with new abilities: Many areas are blocked until you acquire specific tools or skills later in the game.
  • Use photo mode: If available, use the free camera to scout around corners and above ledges for hidden paths.
