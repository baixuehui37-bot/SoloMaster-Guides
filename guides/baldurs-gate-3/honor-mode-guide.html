Speedrun Optimization Tips

  • Skip cutscenes: Every cutscene you skip saves 30-120 seconds. Over a full run, this adds up to 30+ minutes.
  • Run past trash mobs: Only fight enemies that block your path or drop required items. Running saves hours.
  • Memorize map layouts: Knowing exactly where to go without checking the map saves 5-10 minutes per area.
  • Optimize your route: Complete areas in minimal-visits order. Every backtrack costs minutes.
  • Practice bosses in isolation: Use save backups to practice difficult bosses without replaying the entire section.
