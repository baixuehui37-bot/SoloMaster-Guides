Warning: Relationship choices in Baldur's Gate 3 can lock you out of certain content and affect the ending. If you want to see all content, create a manual save before major dialogue choices. Some relationships are mutually exclusive — pursuing one may permanently close off another.
Pro Tip: The best all-around party composition is one tank (draws aggro), one DPS (kills things), one support (heals/buffs), and one flex slot (either second DPS for easy content or second support for hard content). Avoid parties with no tank — enemies will target your damage dealers and you will spend more time healing than fighting.
