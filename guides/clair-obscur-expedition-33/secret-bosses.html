Pro Tip: The #1 mistake players make against bosses in Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 is being too aggressive during the boss's neutral stance. Respect the pattern. Attack only during confirmed recovery windows. If you are unsure whether you can get a hit in, you cannot. Wait for the next opening.

Common Boss Attack Patterns to Master

  • Sweep Attack: Jump or dodge backward. Do NOT dodge sideways — the hitbox is wider horizontally.
  • Thrust/Lunge: Dodge sideways. Do NOT dodge backward — the attack tracks forward movement.
  • Overhead Slam: Dodge sideways, then immediately attack. The boss has a long recovery animation.
  • Grab Attack: Stop attacking immediately. Run/dodge twice in any direction. Grabs have hyper armor.
  • Area of Effect (AoE): Sprint to the edge of the arena. You cannot i-frame through most AoE attacks.
  • Summon Adds: Switch to AoE weapon/ability. Clear adds before focusing boss again. Ignored adds = death.
