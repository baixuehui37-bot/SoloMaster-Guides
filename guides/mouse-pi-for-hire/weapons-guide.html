Mouse: P.I. For Hire — Complete Weapon Arsenal: DPS Tier List & Acquisition Guide

Mouse: P.I. For Hire's weapon sandbox is deceptively deep. Behind the 1930s cartoon aesthetic lies a shooter with 24 weapons, each with unique fire modes, upgrade paths, and hidden interactions. This guide ranks every weapon by actual DPS (tested on the Chapter 3 boss, "Big Tony" Marcelli, on Hard difficulty), explains how to get the secret upgrades, and tells you which guns are worth your hard-earned cheese coins.

Pro Tip: The Thompson Annihilator's "Spray Pattern" upgrade (cost: 450 Cheese Coins at Mickey's Gun Shop, Ch. 4) spreads bullets in a horizontal fan. At close range (<8m), each bullet hits a separate hitbox, effectively tripling your damage against large enemies like the Sewer Gator boss. This is the single highest DPS weapon in the game against large targets.

Warning: The Art Deco Cannon is a one-shot weapon with a 5-second reload. If you miss, you're defenseless for 5 full seconds. Only use it when you have a secondary weapon with quick-swap unlocked (Ch. 4 perk, 300 Coins). Never equip it as your only weapon.
Pro Tip: The Gangster's Cane Shotgun is the single most underrated weapon in the game. It bypasses every "No Weapons" checkpoint (hotel, casino, courthouse), letting you bring a shotgun into areas designed for fistfights. In the Chapter 3 casino ambush, this turns a difficult encounter into a 30-second room clear.
