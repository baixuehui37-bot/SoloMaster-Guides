Resource Management: Ammo, Herbs & Crafting

Resident Evil Requiem is the most resource-scarce RE game since the original RE2 remake. On Standard difficulty, you'll find roughly 60% less ammunition than in RE4 Remake. Every bullet counts.

ResourceOptimal UseNever Do This
Handgun AmmoHeadshots on basic zombies, leg shots for stagger → knife killBody shots (waste), shooting Lickers (immune to handgun frontal damage)
Shotgun ShellsLickers (one-shot from behind), boss weak points, crowd control when corneredSingle zombies at range (waste), Tyrant-type enemies (barely scratches them)
Magnum RoundsBoss damage phases ONLY. You get ~18 rounds total on StandardAnything that isn't a boss. Every Magnum round used on a zombie is a boss attempt lost
Green HerbCombined with Red Herb for full heal (G+R = 100% HP)Using alone (G only heals 25%). Always combine
Red HerbAlways combine with Green. Never use alone (zero healing alone)Discarding — you get exactly enough for the game's hardest fights
Blue HerbCombined with G+R for damage resistance buff (90 seconds, 30% damage reduction)Using alone unless poisoned (rare). The G+R+B combo is S-tier for boss fights
GunpowderCraft handgun ammo (10 powder = 5 rounds) or shotgun shells (20 powder = 3 shells)Hoarding until endgame. Craft as you go — inventory space is more valuable than holding powder
Chem FluidCombine with herbs for First Aid Spray (heals to full, 1 inventory slot = 3 sprays worth)Discarding. First Aid Spray is 3x more inventory-efficient than loose herbs
Pro Tip: The single most important inventory rule: knife kills save 5-8 bullets per zombie. Shoot a zombie in the leg once → it staggers → knife it on the ground. This uses 1 bullet instead of 5-7 headshots. Over the course of the game, this saves roughly 300 handgun rounds — enough for an entire extra playthrough.

Major Puzzle Solutions (Quick Reference)

RPD Statue Puzzle (3F, Main Hall balcony): Lion → Leaf → Bird. Reward: Medallion (needed for underground exit).

Library Bookshelf Puzzle: Move shelves in order: leftmost → rightmost → center. The correct order is shown by the painting on the second floor — trace the arrows from left to right.

Sewer Chess Plug Puzzle: King (Water) → Queen (Fire) → Bishop (Life) → Rook (Death). Plug into corresponding slots. King goes in the slot with the crown icon.

Greenhouse Control Room: Code is 2048 (written on the whiteboard behind the dead researcher). Enter on the keypad to unlock herbicide.

NEST Lab Vaccine Puzzle: Combine blue vial + yellow vial → shake → add red catalyst → inject. The correct temperature is 37.2°C (shown on the terminal).

Pro Tip: G-Birkin Stage 3 has a hidden cheese strategy. Before the fight, craft 6 Acid Rounds for the Grenade Launcher. This effectively skips Phase 2.

Speedrun Tips: S+ Rank Route (Under 2 Hours 30 Minutes)

  1. Skip the Shotgun. It saves 4 minutes of backtracking. The Magnum + knife combo is enough damage for the entire run.
  2. RPD Courtyard skip: After extinguishing the helicopter fire, immediately go to the Library. If you move fast enough, Mr. X won't spawn until you reach the Clock Tower — saving 15 minutes of evasion.
  3. Grace sections: Shadow Step past every enemy in the Sewers. You can complete Grace's entire Sewer segment without firing a single shot (saves 25+ handgun rounds for Leon's boss fights).
  4. G-Birkin Stage 2 skip: You can skip the crane entirely. Stand at the far end of the platform, wait for his charge attack, dodge at the last frame — he falls off the platform automatically. Saves 5 minutes.
  5. Final boss: Use the Rocket Launcher. The rocket launcher spawns at 3:00 on the fight timer. Run laps until it appears → one-shot the boss. This is the only way to get S+ rank on the final fight.
