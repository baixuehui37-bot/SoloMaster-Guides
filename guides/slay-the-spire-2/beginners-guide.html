Top 10 Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Button mashing: Combat requires deliberate, timed inputs. Random mashing gets you killed.
  2. Ignoring tutorials: The game teaches you exactly what you need. Read every tutorial popup.
  3. Running past enemies: You need the XP and loot from early encounters to handle later challenges.
  4. Hoarding consumables: Use potions, buffs, and special items. The game gives you plenty more.
  5. Sticking to one weapon: Different encounters favor different tools. Build a versatile loadout.
  6. Skipping side content: Side quests provide essential XP, gear, and story context.
  7. Ignoring stats: Read what each stat does. A balanced character is better than a min-maxed one for beginners.
  8. Panic rolling/dodging: Spamming dodge puts you in recovery frames when the attack actually lands.
  9. Not reading item descriptions: Many items have hidden effects explained only in their descriptions.
  10. Giving up too soon: Difficult games require persistence. Every death teaches you something.
